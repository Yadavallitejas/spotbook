Privacy Policy

At SpotBook, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details (name, phone number, email address)
  • Booking history and service preferences
  • Payment details (processed securely by third-party providers)
  • Location data (only with your permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and manage our services
  • Communicate with you about bookings and offers
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud

3. Sharing of Information

We never sell your data. We only share your information with:

  • Service professionals to fulfill your bookings
  • Payment gateways for secure transactions
  • Authorities, when required by law

4. Data Security

We take security seriously. Our systems are protected with encryption, and we ensure third-party providers also maintain strong security measures.

5. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data anytime by visiting your account settings or contacting us directly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
